Security Assessment Methodology

All testing is conducted under written authorization and follows strict ethical and legal guidelines.

Technical Security Assessment Approach

1. Reconnaissance & Information Gathering

  • Subdomain enumeration and external attack surface mapping
  • Technology stack fingerprinting (CMS, frameworks, cloud services)
  • API endpoint discovery and documentation review
  • DNS, network, and infrastructure enumeration
  • Identification of exposed services and misconfigurations

2. Vulnerability Assessment

  • Automated scanning with strict manual validation
  • Business logic flaw identification
  • Authentication and authorization testing
  • Input validation, injection, and deserialization testing
  • Cloud configuration and access control review

3. Exploitation & Impact Validation

  • Safe proof‑of‑concept execution under authorization
  • Privilege escalation and lateral movement testing
  • WAF and security control bypass evaluation
  • Data exposure and business risk validation

4. Risk Translation

  • Mapping technical findings to business impact
  • Service disruption and reputational risk assessment
  • Financial and operational impact analysis

5. Remediation & Hardening Support

  • Vendor coordination and responsible disclosure
  • Secure configuration and patch guidance
  • WAF and access control tuning recommendations
  • Validation of applied fixes
